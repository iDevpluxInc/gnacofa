Executive Summary

This concept by Ghana National Cocoa Farmers Association (GNACOFA) has been developed to provide information to prospective investors. It provides information on the concept, overview of the cocoa industry in Ghana, and the proposed shareholding structure. The information contained in this document shall form the basis information of a detailed business plan to circulate to all prospective shareholders.

The Association was incorporated and registered with the company registration Act 1963 (Act 179) on operational division in seeking welfare of its members. Foster unity among members, providing loans by GNACOFA Co-operative Credit Union Ltd to its members providently or productive purposes at fair competitive rate of interest, promoting progress of cocoa business and advance development in the farming communities, training member on new and better agricultural techniques to improve cocoa farming in the country.

GNACOFA obtained Co-operative Credit Union operational license certificate on 1st November, 2017 registered under Paragraph 5(1) of the Co-operative Societies Decree No. 252 of 1968 as the Ghana National Cocoa Farmers' Co-operative Credit Union Limited.
